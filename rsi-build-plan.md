README: Retail Sites International (RSI) Website Rebuild
Project Overview
Objective: Rebuild the digital presence for Retail Sites International, Inc. to establish them as the premier partner in commercial real estate and asset management. The new site will replace their legacy Wix platform with a modern, high-performance, responsive web application.
Tool: Anti Gravity
Target Audience: National tenants, REITs, local developers, landowners, and franchisees looking for site selection, tenant representation, and 1031 exchanges.

Design System & Architecture
Vibe: Authoritative, Established, Premium, and Trustworthy.

Color Palette: * Primary: Oxford Blue (#0B1D3A) - Represents trust, corporate stability, and professionalism.

Secondary: Slate Grey (#64748B) - Modern, structural accent.

Accent: Champagne Gold or Brass (#C5A059) - Denotes high-value transactions and premium service.

Background: Crisp White (#FFFFFF) and Light Ash (#F8F9FA).

Typography: * Headings: A modern serif (e.g., Playfair Display or Merriweather) to convey legacy and authority.

Body: A clean, highly legible sans-serif (e.g., Inter or Roboto) for modern readability.
